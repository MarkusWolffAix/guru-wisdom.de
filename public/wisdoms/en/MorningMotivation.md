# The Winning Day
## A Morning Manifesto

The core themes of the lyrics form a clear blueprint for a successful morning: deep gratitude immediately upon waking, consciously setting goals and intentions, building physical and mental strength through exercise and healthy eating, and strictly protecting oneself from negative influences such as news and social media in the early morning.

The subconscious mind is effectively programmed for focus, gratitude, and a proactive mindset directly from sleep. For the past few years, I've been woken up by this song on Spotify irregularly and seemingly at random.
