# The Om Symbol
## A Journey Through Consciousness

The Om symbol is far more than just a beautiful symbol—it's like a loving map of your own consciousness. It gently reminds us that we are so much more than just the noise of our everyday lives.

Here is a summary of the wonderful insights into the facets of your being:

## The Facets of Your Being

* **The Waking State (Jagrat):** The large curve in the lower left represents your daily experience in the tangible world. It is the here and now, in which you feel, act, and experience life in all its colors with your senses.

* **The Dream State (Svapna):** The gentle curve on the right embraces your inner world of thoughts. Here, you are free from the limitations of physics and create your own subtle realities in dreams or imagination.

* **Deep Sleep (Sushupti):** The upper left curve is like a soft, protective blanket. In this formless state of profound, dreamless stillness, your mind can completely let go. Everything comes to a standstill, and you can find pure, undisturbed trust and rest.

* **The Veil of Illusion (Maya):** The delicate crescent moon symbolizes the shimmering of the material world. It lovingly shows us that our everyday worries and perceptions are often merely fleeting illusions that slightly obscure our view of what truly matters.

* **Pure Consciousness (Turiya):** The luminous point at the very top is your true essence. It is the absolute, silent observer consciousness, completely untouched by time, space, or the storms of life. It whispers to you: Behind all the hustle and bustle of the world and the dreams of your mind, you are perfect, peaceful, and whole.

## The Realization

When we contemplate Om or feel its sound, we embark on precisely this journey: We wander through our everyday experiences, gently penetrate the veil of illusion, and find our way back to our own unshakable peace.
