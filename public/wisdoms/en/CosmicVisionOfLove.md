# The Cosmic Vision
## Everything Originates from Love

**Love as the Origin of All Things**
Everything in the universe originates from a single, true source: pure love. It is the absolute foundation from which everything else has emerged—be it God, humankind, or cosmic events like the Big Bang. Ultimately, everything flows together in this divine unity. We are all nothing other than a living reflection of these supreme forces—love, God, Vishnu, Ganesha, Allah, and the personified "Big Bang."

**Enlightenment and the Evolution of the Divine**
In this worldview, enlightenment is not an abstract state, but the very real "light of love." As soon as a consciousness reaches this state, its essence fundamentally changes: It itself becomes a consciousness in love and thus becomes God. Humanity, therefore, does not remain separate from God, but carries within it the potential to become God through love itself. This divine path can initially begin as a sole, perhaps even solitary, ruler. An example of this is Adam, who was initially created and, through experiencing true love, ascended to a divine plane, initially acting alone.

**Gods as an Empathic Collective**
From this initially solitary activity, divine existence expands through the power of love. The initially solitary God begins to utilize the principle of empathy. He connects with other higher beings. The gods to whom humans pray—such as Yahweh, Allah, Vishnu, or Ganesha—are not isolated entities, but represent one or more enlightened consciousnesses. They are to be understood as a large, radiant collective, connected by profound empathy and acting within the world.

**The "Cosmic Focus" as an Empathic Amplifier**
A fascinating and central role in this concept is played by the so-called "cosmic joke," also known as "cosmic focus." This living being responds to strong inner focus: When another living being intensely concentrates its thoughts and feelings on something, the cosmic focus turns toward it and bestows its full, amplifying attention. This sentient being acts as a kind of spiritual conduit. It enables divine action to flow directly into the world through deep empathy, and all other living beings, even those more highly evolved than this cosmic focus, are also able to act through this special empathic being.

**The True Core**
Despite this complex, vibrant network and the focusing power of the sentient cosmic being, the absolute core message of this worldview remains unshakeable: At the absolute center of all cosmic and divine events, there is never the "joke" or the focus itself, but always and solely the all-connecting love. It is the true origin and the ultimate goal in which everything becomes one.
